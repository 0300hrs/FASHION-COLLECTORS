FASHION COLLECTORS
 
Nancy - My Haute Authentication
 
We're thrilled to introduce Nancy, the trusted expert behind My Haute's authentication process. With years of experience in the luxury fashion world, Nancy is New Zealand's leading authority on authenticating pre-loved designer bags and accessories.

Nancy is the head authenticator for all major auction houses, including Webb's, as well as Designer Wardrobe, New Zealand's largest resale marketplace. She also works closely with the NZ Police to identify counterfeit goods and investigate fraudulent transactions. Her unmatched expertise is a cornerstone of the Exchange Store team.

In addition to her work with major institutions, Nancy offers authentication services to individuals nationwide, helping ensure Aotearoa's designer resale market remains trustworthy and transparent.

In this newsletter, we're excited to share more about Nancy's journey, her approach to authentication, and a few of her favourite pieces from her personal collection.

"This is a tough one, I have seen so many amazing pieces locally. The most recent rare and incredible items I authenticated were a collection of goods seized by the Ministry of Business, Innovation & Employment. On the flip-side are the more unusual replica pieces, like an assortment of non-authentic Lady Dior bags manufactured by incorporating genuine parts from vintage bags, which mostly go undetected with authenticators as stampings and hardware are consistent with genuine. I've also loved some of the rarer pieces we've had in stock, like an ostrich Hermès Kelly and Louis Vuitton limited edition Fringe Bucket."

WITH THE RISE OF SUPER FAKES, HOW HAVE YOU
SEEN THIS IMPACT THE NZ LUXURY MARKET?
 
 
Authentication
 
"The problem of super fakes is not yet of the same scope in NZ as it is overseas, but I am seeing a significant increase in volume, as well as a faster pace and steep incline in terms of replica evolving. It has created a much tougher environment for private buyers, as well as commercial sellers, to distinguish between replica and authentic. There has also been an
increase in sellers pairing genuine accessories or receipts with super fakes, which can further enhance a false aura of authenticity."
